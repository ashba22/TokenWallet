How to Use TokenWallet?

Step 1

Register for an Account to access TokenWallet's features. Provide your basic information and create a secure login password.

Step 2

Purchase Tokens with ease using PayPal or your Credit Card. Tokens serve as the digital currency within TokenWallet.

Step 3

Utilize Tokens for Payment of Services. We support a variety of services that can be paid for using your tokens.

Additionally, TokenWallet can automatically suggest and pay for nearby services based on your GPS location, adding even more convenience to your transactions.
